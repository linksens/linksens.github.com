Wireless networking system for power management

This wireless networking system for power management is used to monitor various kinds of electrical equipments. It employs sensors to collect environmental information consisted of temperature, humidity, carbon dioxide, moving objects, etc. The power consumption and environmental status data can be transmitted to LinkSens Web of management platform from those sensors. Then users will be able to remote control electrical equipments such as air conditioning system via the web or mobile devices easily. This dynamic energy management can make electrical equipments humanized and more efficient.


Main applications:
- Industrial remote control, telemetry, automated data collection, room temperature and humidity control, real-time image monitoring;
- Access control systems, personnel location, intelligent control of the family, baby monitor;
- Traffic and street light control, logistics, active RFID system, security systems;
- Wireless mouse, wireless handsets, wireless speakers, wireless headset, wireless explaining device, wireless electrophonic musical instrument, wireless monitoring, wireless meter reading, wireless car rearview;
